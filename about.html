About Us

The VoltWorks e-Powertrain system enhances driving performance with optimized products that maximize efficiency. Its advanced technology and expertise in electric vehicles also produce a quieter, smoother and more pleasant driving experience. The electric drive motor is a crucial element in electric vehicles and plays a major role in determining driving performance. Unlike internal combustion engines, which rely on heat to increase RPM, the drive motor uses current to control torque and can produce maximum torque from the start. This results in a smoother, quieter, and more comfortable driving experience. The VoltWorks e-Powertrain offers optimal drive motor solutions to global automakers, utilizing superior design and manufacturing technology to meet a range of needs. Welcome to VoltWorks, the leading provider of powertrain solutions for electric vehicles. We are dedicated to designing, developing, and manufacturing innovative powertrain systems that deliver exceptional performance, efficiency, and reliability. Our team of experts has extensive experience in the electric vehicle industry, and we use the latest technology and techniques to create cutting-edge powertrain systems. Whether you're an automaker looking for a custom powertrain solution for your next EV model, or a fleet operator looking to convert your vehicles to electric, we have the expertise and capabilities to meet your needs. Our powertrain solutions are designed to deliver maximum efficiency and range, while minimizing environmental impact. And with our advanced control systems, you can enjoy smooth and responsive acceleration, even in the most demanding driving conditions. If you're looking for a partner to help you take your electric vehicle program to the next level, look no further than VoltWorks. Contact us today to learn more about how we can help you achieve your EV goals.
