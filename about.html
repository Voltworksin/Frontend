We are committed to be at the forefront of innovation in the electric vehicle industry, with a mission to develop electric vehicle skateboards. Oxygen Plant & an EV Prototype

The team has developed a 600 LPM Oxygen Plant during 2nd wave of COVID-19 in India in record time of 60 days. The team has also developed India’s most energy-efficient EV prototype and its 1kW BLDC motor controller.

Floating CNG & a Solar Energy Station

The team has developed world’s first floating CNG station, deployed at the NAMO Ghat in Varanasi. The team has also built India’s first RCC-based floating Solar Energy Station.

Clusterline Balzers System

One of the team members was involved in the development of CLUSTERLINE® 300, which is the first PVD tool worldwide that runs 300mm thin wafer backside metallization for power devices in production.
